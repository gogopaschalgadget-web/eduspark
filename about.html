About EduSpark

Free educational games and tools built to make learning fun for every kid, every grade.

Our Mission

EduSpark exists to make learning genuinely fun for K-8 students. We build free interactive games, quizzes, and study tools that kids actually want to use — covering math, reading, science, and more.

Every game and activity on EduSpark is designed to align with grade-level learning standards while keeping engagement first. We believe that when kids enjoy learning, they learn more.

Who We Are

EduSpark is built and maintained by Paschal Digital — a small team passionate about creating better educational resources for kids and families.

We are independent and ad-supported. We do not collect personal data from children and take student privacy seriously.
