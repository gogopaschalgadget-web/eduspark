Our Mission

EduSpark exists to make K-8 learning genuinely fun. We publish free, curriculum-aligned games, worksheets, and study guides designed to help students in kindergarten through 8th grade build strong foundations in math, reading, science, and writing.

Every resource on EduSpark is built around one question: will a real student find this useful? We do not publish filler content. We focus on what works: clear explanations, engaging formats, and grade-level accuracy.

Parents trust EduSpark because our resources align with Common Core and state standards. Teachers share EduSpark pages because the content actually reinforces classroom lessons. Students return because learning here does not feel like work.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is EduSpark free to use?

Yes. All core resources on EduSpark are completely free. You do not need an account to access worksheets, guides, or game recommendations. We keep the site running through display advertising.

Are the resources aligned with state standards?

Yes. We align content to Common Core State Standards (CCSS) and cross-reference grade-level benchmarks for math and English Language Arts. Science content references Next Generation Science Standards (NGSS) where applicable.

Can teachers use EduSpark resources in class?

Absolutely. Teachers are welcome to print and share EduSpark worksheets and guides for classroom use at no cost. We ask only that you do not republish or resell our content as your own.

How often is new content added?

We publish new guides, worksheets, and resource lists every week. Check our blog for the latest additions.
