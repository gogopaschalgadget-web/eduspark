Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

EduSpark ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website at gogopaschalgadget-web.github.io/eduspark/. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our site.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

EduSpark is designed for K-8 students and takes children's privacy seriously. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Our site does not require account creation or any personal data submission to use our games and tools.

If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at gogopaschalgadget@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

Information We Collect

We do not require account creation or personal information to use EduSpark. However, the following data may be collected automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time on site, browser type, device type, and referring URL — collected via Google Analytics (GA4).
  • Cookies: We use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can opt out via your browser settings or Google's opt-out tools.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use our site. Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

Google AdSense & Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. We have configured our AdSense settings to serve non-personalized ads where applicable for audiences that may include children.

Google and its partners may use cookies to serve ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising at aboutads.info.

By using this site, you consent to cookies for advertising as described in Google's Privacy & Terms at policies.google.com/privacy.

Third-Party Links

EduSpark may link to third-party educational resources or retailers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Parents should supervise children's use of external links.

Affiliate Disclosure

EduSpark may earn commissions from affiliate links, including Amazon Associates. This does not affect the price you pay.

Data Security

We do not store personal data on our servers. All analytics and advertising data is handled by Google in accordance with their security standards.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
