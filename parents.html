Frequently Asked Questions

Is EduSpark really free?

Yes! EduSpark is completely free with no hidden fees, premium tiers, or subscriptions. We believe quality education should be accessible to all families.

How is EduSpark different from other learning apps?

EduSpark is created by educators and built with privacy-first principles. We're COPPA compliant, completely ad-free for kids, and our games are directly aligned with school curricula. We focus on learning outcomes, not engagement metrics.

What grades and subjects are available?

We offer games for Grades 1-5 in Math, Reading, Science, Spelling, Geography, and History. We're continuously expanding our library with new grades and subjects.

Is my child's data safe?

Absolutely. EduSpark is COPPA compliant, meaning we don't collect, use, or share any personal information from children under 13. There's no tracking, no ads targeted at kids, and no data sales.

Can my child play on a tablet or phone?

Yes! All EduSpark games are fully responsive and work on tablets, phones, and computers. You can start a game on one device and continue on another.

Does my child need to create an account?

No account is needed to play games. Simply visit our site, choose a grade and subject, and start learning immediately. No sign-ups, no passwords, no hassle.
